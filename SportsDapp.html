SportsDapp

The details of a tournament were not adequately stated in the match cards for upcoming matches.

The leaderboard's visual appeal with the prize sum and position seemed out of place.

The flow of the quiz context and team contest was unclear to the user.

It was necessary to redefine branding and identification using better styles and colors.

Rebranding SportsDapp

Created a focused color palette with minimal use of accent colors, to shift the user’s attention to key areas of the app; Creating Team & Predictions

Elements that helped user’s easily locate their participated matches and status of predictions made

How the application works

Pick Tournament

You can play upcoming as well as live matches. Click on the match you want to play.

Strategise & Answer

Click on any prediction to answer them. Both general & private rooms exist. Choose the best possible option you think of. You can also check the live scenarios of participation.

Form Teams

Create your best 11 team for the match. Participate in contests and stand a chance to win big.

Takeaway & Learning

I realised constant discussion and testing specific flows was key to the design process. It might be difficult to completly create new interactions but identifying what does not work answers the design question.
