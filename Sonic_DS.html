Introduction

Working on a scaling product, we found ourselves in a chaotic environment where we blame other teams for bad quality of output and messy design implementation on shipping products that also easily transitions into hostile interactions between design and dev teams during handoffs. A design system is an instrument that bridges this gap and brings harmony within teams.

Problem Statement

There was a lot of inconsistency across various UI components (which often lead to failure of brand recognition) in the long run. Designers were constantly investing a lot of time in reinventing and reconstructing different components for the same functionality and requirement, we had so many variations, even for simple components like a button adding further complexity and ambiguity to the system.
